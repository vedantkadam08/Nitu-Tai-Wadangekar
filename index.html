Grassroots Leadership

Nitu Tai Wadangekar serves as the Mumbai Mahila President for the Nationalist Congress Party (Sharadchandra Pawar). Her leadership is defined by an unwavering loyalty to the vision of Sharad Pawar and the core values of the Maha Vikas Aghadi (MVA).

As a mobilizer on the ground, she focuses on bridging the gap between policy and the people, ensuring that the struggles of Mumbai's working-class women are heard at the highest levels of governance.

"Loyalty to the Ideology, Service to the People."

Key Issues & Advocacy

Women’s Rights & Safety

Championing the safety of women in Mumbai's public spaces and expanding access to legal resources.

Combating Inflation

Addressing the rising cost of essential goods that impacts the daily lives of Mumbai's families.

Social Welfare

Working within the MVA framework to ensure government welfare schemes reach the marginalized sectors.
