A Grassroots Mobilizer

Nitu Tai Wadangekar has dedicated her life to the principles of social justice and equality. As a staunch loyalist to Shri Sharad Pawar Saheb, she has consistently fought for the marginalized, ensuring that the voice of the common Mumbaikar reaches the highest corridors of power.

Her journey is one of resilience and unwavering commitment to the NCP (SP) ideology. From local neighborhood circles to leading the Mahila wing in the heart of Mumbai, her leadership is defined by action, empathy, and organizational excellence.
